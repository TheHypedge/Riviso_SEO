Privacy Policy

How Auto Articles collects, uses, and protects your information.

Last updated: {{ (now() if now is defined else '') }}

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Auto Articles (“we”, “us”) collects and uses information when you use the Auto Articles application and related services (the “Service”).

2. Information we collect

  • Account information: email address, name (if provided), and authentication metadata.
  • Project data: project names, website URLs, WordPress connection settings you provide.
  • Content you generate: article drafts, prompts, keywords, and scheduling metadata.
  • Usage & diagnostics: basic logs used for security, debugging, and performance.

3. How we use information

  • Provide and operate the Service (generate content, schedule posts, publish to WordPress).
  • Maintain security, prevent abuse, and troubleshoot issues.
  • Improve the product experience and reliability.

4. Cookies & analytics

We may use cookies and similar technologies to remember preferences and (if you consent) to measure usage using analytics. You can control analytics via the cookie consent banner.

5. Data sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with service providers strictly to operate the Service, such as hosting, storage, and API providers.

6. WordPress connections

If you connect WordPress, the Service uses the credentials you provide to call the WordPress REST API to publish posts and upload media. You can disconnect or update these settings anytime in your project settings.

7. Data retention

We keep your data for as long as your account remains active or as needed to provide the Service. You can request deletion of your account and associated data.

8. Contact

For privacy questions, contact us at {{ support_email|default('support@autoarticles.local') }}.
