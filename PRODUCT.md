# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Content writers, SEO freelancers, marketing managers, agency operators, and solo founders — anyone managing SEO content operations at any scale. Their context when they open Riviso: they need to produce, publish, and track articles efficiently. The primary job is "get quality content out to my WordPress site and know if it's working."

## Product Purpose

Riviso is an AI-assisted content operations platform. It generates SEO articles, publishes them to WordPress, and surfaces Google Search Console performance data — all in one place. Success looks like: fewer browser tabs, less copy-paste, more published content, and visible ROI on what they wrote.

## Brand Personality

Calm authority. Riviso is the capable colleague in the room, not the one pitching you. Three words: **precise, considered, reliable**. Emotions: confidence (I know this will work), focus (no distractions), quiet satisfaction (things are done and done right).

## Anti-references

Hyped startup aesthetic: gradient text, glassmorphism, big hero animations, "AI-powered" badge everywhere, oversized stat cards, breathless copy. Riviso should feel like a tool built by people who use it — not a landing page for a funding round. The product's credibility comes from what it does, not from visual excitement.

Also avoid: enterprise grey (Jira-grey, dense nav trees, modal-heavy flows) and generic SaaS cream (warm-neutral bg, identical card grids, hero metrics, indistinguishable from every other 2024-2026 AI tool).

## Design Principles

1. **Disappear into the work** — The interface's job is to get out of the way. Every screen should feel obvious on first contact. No decorative chrome; every element earns its place.
2. **Editorial precision** — Typography and hierarchy are doing real work here. Whitespace is deliberate, not accidental. The rhythm of the page is considered, like a well-designed publication, not a template.
3. **Earned confidence** — Trust comes from consistency and correctness, not from bold claims. Error messages are specific. Empty states explain next steps. Status is always legible.
4. **Quiet authority** — The warm orange accent (`#d97757`) is the brand's voice — used sparingly for focus, not decoration. When something is highlighted, it means something.
5. **Scale gracefully** — The same tool serves a solo blogger and a 20-project agency. Density and information hierarchy must work at both ends.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA as the minimum bar. Body text ≥4.5:1 contrast against background. Interactive elements have clear focus states. Reduced motion respected. No color as the sole information channel (pair color with label or icon). No assumptions about screen size — responsive from 375px to ultrawide.
